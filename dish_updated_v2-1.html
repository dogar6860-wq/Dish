Installation Tips
Azimuth — horizontal rotation. Use live compass tab to align precisely.
Elevation — vertical tilt from ground level. Tilt dish up to this angle.
LNB Skew — rotate LNB clockwise (+) or anti-clockwise (−) by the shown degrees.
Stick length affects LNB focal point. Use the calculated offset to position LNB correctly.
Fine tune — after rough alignment, peak signal with receiver signal meter in small sweeps.
